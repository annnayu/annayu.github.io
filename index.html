OH HEY THIS IS About

Hey there! I’m Anna Yu.

A curious product designer fascinated about designing digital products that enrich people’s lives.

From Sept 2014 to May 2019

I specalized in Urban Design, at the University of Waterloo.

From my academic background I learned how to approach problems in a holistic way and design at a human scale.

Along the way, I grew an interest in designing for digital interfaces.

I noticed there were many pain points, specifically within the planning industry, where the collection of information or design processes can be significantly improved if certain digital platforms and tools existed.

Right now, I’m focused on growing as a designer and honing my skills in product thinking, interaction design, and visual design.
